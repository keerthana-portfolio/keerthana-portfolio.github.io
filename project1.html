Case Study

Why Food Battery is created?

Food Battery is created for a popular restaurant to provide maximum food dining with less crowded environment. Here the customer requirements can be clearly noticed when the need customized food.

The Challenges

The main challenge is to collect information from the kitchen department in the restaurant to provide multiple customizing options

The Obstacles

The main obstacles was changing the dining environment to provide great experience.

Solution

Providing small changes by placing QR code card for each table, placing food order and payment monitoring devices and training staffs for the changes.

Results

Crowd in dining environment is reduced. Customers are provided with more options in food requirement. Number of customers coming to the Restaurant is increased.
