Food Order App on Subscription Basis

Customer Side

Problem Statement

There are some food delivery apps, people can buy foods when they need it. but what if working professionals and college students requires food for a monthly subscription. Imagine some users need food three times a day in a monthly or weekly subscription, on the other side there are hotels, mess, and home-cooked food providers who can provide the food for a monthly subscription. come up with the solution by creating an application that connects the needful customers to the food providers.

Disclaimer: This problem statement is my interview test problem in Hakunamatata Solutions.
