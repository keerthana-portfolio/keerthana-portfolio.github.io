Flower Facts Blog

Case Study

Why Flower Facts is created?

Flower Facts is created to spread some interesting facts and details about flowers we daily use and rarely blossomed and expensive to buy.

The Challenges

The main challenge is to collect correct details from books, articles, and other websites.

Why Wordpress?

WordPress is, by far, the most popular open source Content Management System (CMS). WordPress is free to install, deploy, and upgrade. Thousands of plugins and templates power a flexible and simple interface, which reduces development costs and deployment time. Since it is a startup we find that Wordpress as the ideal solution because of the ability to create a great experience for content creators while also providing an enterprise-grade core platform to make further development more efficient with the goal of maximizing ad revenue.

The Obstacles

The main obstacles was collecting stock images for some flowers because there were only very few related images in online platform and selecting the right theme for the website.

Solution

Surfing and discussions with the team member was the best solution to provide an optimal website.

Results

By project completion, Flower Facts provides great details and interesting facts about different kinds of flowers around the World.

Basic main users

  • Mostly the users can be, students studying Botany.
  • Beginners in plant researching.
  • People with a habit of exploring new things,etc.
